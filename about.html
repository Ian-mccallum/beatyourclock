Our Story

Beat the Clock was founded by Ian McCallum with a clear mission: to help small businesses reclaim their most valuable resource - time. Through automation, we aim to transform how businesses handle their day-to-day operations.

With extensive experience in process automation and a deep understanding of tools like Zapier and Make.com, Ian saw an opportunity to help small businesses leverage the power of automation without the complexity and overhead typically associated with digital transformation.
